Communication Skills

Communication Skills is a professional development course that helps students improve verbal, written, interpersonal and presentation abilities. The subject focuses on spoken English, technical writing, business communication, professional etiquette, teamwork and leadership communication. Strong communication skills are essential for engineers, developers, entrepreneurs, researchers and technology professionals working in modern organizations.
