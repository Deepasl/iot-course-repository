Digital Logic Design

Digital Logic Design is the foundation of modern electronic systems, microprocessors, embedded devices and IoT hardware. This course teaches students number systems, Boolean algebra, logic gates, combinational circuits, sequential circuits, flip-flops, counters and digital system design. Understanding digital logic is essential for building smart devices, embedded controllers and automation systems.
